# Dade-Collier Airport Flight Monitoring

Automated monitoring of flight activity at Dade-Collier Training and Transition Airport (TNT), also known as "Alligator Alcatraz" - a facility used for ICE detention and migrant transportation operations.

## Purpose
Track charter flight patterns to document migrant transportation operations and build evidentiary record for accountability journalism and potential litigation.

## Data Collection
- Source: OpenSky Network (public ADS-B data)
- Frequency: Every 30 minutes
- Coverage: 10km radius around airport
- Logged: Aircraft ID, altitude, timestamp, flight status
